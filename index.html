About Me.

Dr. Hima Bindu is a visionary academic leader dedicated to shaping the future of education. As the Dean of Training & Placements at CMR Group of Institutions, she has been instrumental in designing strategic training programs that equip students with industry-relevant skills. With a strong background in Human Resources Management and a PhD from Osmania University, she brings a research-driven approach to curriculum development, ensuring students are prepared for the evolving job market.

With over 18 years of experience, Dr. Hima Bindu has successfully built strong industry collaborations, facilitating placements in top-tier companies. She is committed to enhancing student employability through innovative learning strategies, career counseling, and skill development initiatives. Under her leadership, the training and placement department continues to achieve remarkable success, shaping the careers of aspiring professionals from CMR Group of Institutions.
